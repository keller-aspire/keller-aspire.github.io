### Bio

Qihong Wang is a senior research analyst with 20 years of laboratory experience in the United States. He has extensive expertise in animal survival surgery and microsurgical techniques across multiple species, including mice, rats, rabbits, guinea pigs, and swine. Throughout his career, he has developed and executed robust in vivo models of cardiac arrest, hemorrhagic shock and resuscitation, extracorporeal cardiopulmonary resuscitation (eCPR), and solid organ transplantation, supporting both mechanistic studies and translational therapeutic evaluation.
