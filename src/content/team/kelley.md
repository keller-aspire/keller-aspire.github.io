## About Kelley Kempski Leadingham
Kelley M. Kempski Leadingham is a Research Program Manager for the APIRE Lab, where she oversees multidisciplinary research initiatives. She earned her Bachelor of Biomedical Engineering from the University of Delaware in 2018 and her Master of Science in Biomedical Engineering from Johns Hopkins University in 2021. Her research interests include medical image analysis and medical device development, with a focus on translating engineering innovations into clinically impactful solutions. Outside of her work, her hobbies include reading, sewing, and playing the piano.
